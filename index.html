A funny story

Talk about a funny story in one of my basketball games. One hot afternoon we played basketball at my high school. We played basketball with an eighty minute round. Beside the basketball court there is a lake, when we finished playing basketball we went down to the pool and all agreed. After swimming for a while, a friend swam to the middle of the lake, and then he pretended to be drowned, struggling and shouting. And people realized he was choking on water. We told each other to help him but no one came out. I told my friend next door to save him but he said he couldn't swim but actually he went to a swimming competition for the school and won the district consolation prize. I said to Thang:... You are strong and healthy, go out and save him. But he said he's been sick for a few days so he can't come out. I said nothing more, I swam out and saved him, and let him step on me and swim back to shore. When I got ashore, I asked him why he didn't thank me. He asked me "Am I still alive, I thought I was dead then there was no chance to thank you" and he told everyone: "I just swam in the middle of the river and pretended to drown to make fun of everyone. but I couldn't believe my foot was stuck in the rock so I couldn't get to the surface" and he apologized and thanked everyone for their concern and saving him. We laughed a lot then.
