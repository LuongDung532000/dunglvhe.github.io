Football


Soccer is a team sport played with a spherical ball between two teams consisting of 11 players on each side. It has more than 250 million players in more than 200 countries and territories, making it a popular sport. The most popular sport in the world. It plays on a rectangular surface called a football field with a goal at each end. The goal is to score in the opponent's goal. The team with the most goals wins.

Badminton or badminton is a racquet sport played between two players (singles) or two pairs of athletes (doubles) on two halves of a rectangular court divided by a net in the middle. Players score points by sending the ball over the net with a racket and hitting the ground on the other side of the opponent's court. Each side has only 1 touch to bring the ball to the other side. The inning is over when the shuttle hits the ground, or there is an error caught by the umpire or line umpire; In case there is no referee, it is caught by the player himself, at any time during the turn the shuttle is being played.
